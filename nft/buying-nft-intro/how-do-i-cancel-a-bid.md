# How do I cancel a bid?



1. Make sure you are logged in with your account.
2. On the top-right of your screen, press the '**=**' button.
3. Choose '**Notifications**'.
4. Open 'My **Bids**'.&#x20;
5. Press '**Cancel**' next to the corresponding NFT.&#x20;
